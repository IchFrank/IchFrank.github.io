About me

I am an industrial designer with experience in product development and implementation. My experience lies in the field of 3D modeling and the preparation of documentation and technical drawings.


In addition, I have a strong penchant for creative endeavors, suitable for a variety of graphic design responsibilities. This encompasses tasks like conceptualizing and designing logos, shaping brands, selecting engaging presentation styles, orchestrating product communication strategies, and more.


At present, my main objective is aimed at participating in innovative companies. I ardently seek opportunities that allow me to showcase my skills as an exciting career alternative. Through the application of my honed knowledge and proficient skills, I am dedicated to bringing exceptional value to diverse projects, demonstrating my ability to bring innovation and ingenuity to the forefront.
